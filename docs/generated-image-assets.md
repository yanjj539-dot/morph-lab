## Shared art direction

- Mood: a calm, precise independent design laboratory with visible human handling.
- Palette: paper white `#F6F5EF`, pale sky `#BFD4F5`, cobalt `#2456FF`, coral `#FF7157`, near-black `#111318`, brushed aluminum.
- Materials: paper fibers, tracing paper, foam board, matte acrylic, brushed metal, graphite and rubber cable.
- Avoid: purple gradients, neon glow, robots, holograms, glassmorphism, floating spheres, stock-photo polish, fake UI text, logos and watermarks.
